About Me

I am a self-taught Frontend Developer and Medical Physiology graduate pivoting into tech with a focus on logical, high-performance systems. My approach is rooted in the "Swiss Style" philosophy: clarity, order, and uniformity. I believe good design is as little design as possible, allowing content to shine through while ensuring the underlying code remains clean and efficient.

Having transitioned from a background in complex biological systems, I bring a unique analytical perspective to building web interfaces. I have mastered the core fundamentals — HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript — and am currently expanding my technical toolkit to include React and modern architectural frameworks. I am dedicated to creating minimalist, responsive digital experiences that prioritize user intent and functional beauty.
